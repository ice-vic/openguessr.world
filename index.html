How to Play OpenGuessr

Playing OpenGuessr is simple yet challenging. You'll be dropped into a random Street View location anywhere in the world. Use the visual clues around you to determine where you might be - look for signs, architecture, vegetation, terrain, and other geographical indicators.

 

Once you've made your best guess, click on the world map to place your marker. The game will reveal the actual location and award points based on how close your guess was. The closer you are, the more points you earn!

Each round presents a new location and a fresh challenge. Can you identify remote islands, bustling city centers, mountain ranges, or desert landscapes? Put your geography skills to the test and see how well you know our world!
